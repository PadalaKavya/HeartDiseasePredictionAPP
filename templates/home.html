Are you having any of these symptoms ?

In order to avoid any doubts about getting a heart disease, identify the following 4 symptoms of cardio vascular disease:

Unusual Fatigue


Severe weakness, anxiety, fatigue,along with Sweating, upset stomach, vomiting, or dizziness.

Discomfort in chest


Pressure, pain, squeezing, or a sense of fullness in the center of the chest.


Shortness of breath


Feeling shortness of breath leading to Fast or uneven heartbeat.


Shoulder Pain


Pain or discomfort in the shoulder, arm, back, neck, or jaw


Prevention of Heart disease

The success of having a healthy heart is strongly influenced by the strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are several things that can be tried to prevent heart disease to a maximum extent:


  Avoid alcoholic beverages

  Avoid Stress

  Eat Healthy food

  Get enough sleep

  Excercise or physical activity
